Brooks Crossing in Kyle, TX

Making memories

Hidden between Silverado and Brooks Ranch, Brooks Crossing is the kind of neighborhood you only notice if you're looking for it.

With just a handful of streets and almost no through-traffic, life here stays simple and calm. It’s a community where people wave, kids play safely, and the pace slows the moment you turn in.

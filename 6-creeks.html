Information to Consider

  • HOA dues are approximately $800 annually (about $200 quarterly).
  • Estimated ad valorem tax rate: ~1.75% (does not include PID assessment). Example: on a $500,000 home, ad valorem taxes would be roughly $8,750.
  • Six Creeks is in the City of Kyle extraterritorial jurisdiction and receives City water; it is not in a MUD.
  • The community is in a Public Improvement District (PID). The PID uses a fixed annual assessment to fund neighborhood improvements such as roads, parks, and landscaping; that assessment appears as a separate line item from ad valorem taxes.
  • Because Six Creeks uses a PID rather than a MUD, the ad valorem tax rate is lower; homeowners still pay the PID assessment in addition to ad valorem taxes.

Community Specifics in 6 Creeks, Kyle TX

  • Built from 2019 and ongoing
  • 3–5 bedroom homes
  • SqFt: 1,728 sqft to 4,098 sqft
  • Builder: Pulte Homes, Perry Homes, Risewell Homes, Taylor Morrison, Trendmaker Homes, Highland Homes, M/I Homes, and Coventry Homes
  • The community includes amenities like a community pool, pickleball courts, pavilion, trails, fishing pond, and green space.
  • Homes are served by Hays CISD schools (e.g., Cullen Elementary, Barton Middle, Jack C. Hays High).

Steeplechase

A graceful stride into calm

Steeplechase is literally a horse race over a course with obstacles like hedges and jumps. Around here, the race ends where the calm begins.

After you’ve hurdled through another workday and weaved through the obstacles of life, you get to come home to calm — a place where peace and movement coexist. Neighbors stroll by getting their daily steps in, and an active lifestyle feels effortless here.

Within the neighborhood park, you’ll find baseball and soccer fields, a frisbee golf course, playgrounds for the littles, a splash area, walking trails, and basketball courts. Just beyond, three gyms offer even more ways to stay moving. With five different entrances, getting in and out is simple — perfect for avoiding school traffic when needed. It’s a peaceful sanctuary, infused with convenience.

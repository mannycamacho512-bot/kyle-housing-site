The Trails

At The Trails, you set the rules — your home, your way.

Masonwood isn’t just a street. It’s the way you enter The Trails, the road that gently brings you home. Cars pass through, neighbors come and go, and everything moves at an easy, familiar pace.

Once you turn off Masonwood, the quieter streets reveal the heart of the neighborhood, but this road sets the tone. It’s the first impression, the simple transition from the outside world to a place that feels settled and calm.

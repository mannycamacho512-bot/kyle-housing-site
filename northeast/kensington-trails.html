Kensington Trails

Nurtured nook of Kyle

Kensington Trails is a corner of Kyle where childhood unfolds gently and memories grow quietly in the background of everyday life. This is the kind of neighborhood where you’ll one day look back and remember the sweet moments — little feet taking their first steps, wobbly bikes steadied by big hands, and that first day of school that made you more nervous than them.

Here, those moments are woven right into the community. From inside the neighborhood, you can walk your children to Science Hall Elementary — a simple, peaceful routine that becomes part of your family’s story.

If you’re beginning a family or hoping to plant roots in a kind, steady community, this nook of Kyle is a beautiful place to start.

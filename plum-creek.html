Community Specifics in Plum Creek, Kyle TX

  • Southwest section are the Original Plum Creek homes.
  • Next section: The Peninsula at Plum Creek (first named subdivision).
  • Farther north: Plum Creek North — starts around Kohlers Crossing.
  • Above Rikardson: Still Plum Creek North, often called “Lennar at Plum Creek North” since Lennar built those homes.
  • 2,500+ existing single-family homes ranging from $250k to $500k.

Living in Peninsula feels calm, tucked away, and purposeful. This gated enclave within Plum Creek offers a quieter, more private atmosphere, with golf course surroundings that create a relaxed, resort-like rhythm to everyday life.

Morning walks along the trails, afternoons fishing at the waterhole, and evenings watching the light fade over the fairways shape a lifestyle centered on ease and intention. Peninsula is designed for those who value space, simplicity, and low-maintenance living. Residents also enjoy access to Plum Creek’s parks, pools, basketball and pickleball courts, making it easy to balance peaceful living with active options nearby.

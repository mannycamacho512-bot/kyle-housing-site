How to Read Active Listings

Active listings show the supply still competing for buyer attention. On their own, they do not tell you whether a neighborhood is strong or weak. They become more useful when compared against sold volume and days on market.

How to Read Absorption Rate

Absorption rate helps show how quickly demand is working through current inventory. Higher absorption usually means stronger movement relative to supply. Lower absorption can point to slower conditions or growing inventory pressure.

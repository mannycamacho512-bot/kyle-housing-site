What These Charts Mean

The annual charts help you see whether pricing, volume, and pace are changing year over year. The current pressure chart helps show whether the subdivision is converting activity into closings or losing listings along the way. Higher sell-through and absorption tend to reflect stronger movement, while higher failure or a rising withdrawn count can suggest friction.

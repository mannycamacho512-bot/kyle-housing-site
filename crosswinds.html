Information to Consider

  • HOA dues are approximately $575 annually (about $47 per month).
  • Estimated tax rate is around 2.75%. Without exemptions, on a $300,000 home, annual taxes are roughly $8,250.
  • Crosswinds is located in the Kyle, TX ETJ and is served by the Crosswinds Municipal Utility District (MUD). The MUD tax rate is 0.90% (or $0.90 per $100 of assessed value), which increases the overall property tax rate for the neighborhood.
  • Homes are relatively newer, which generally supports a higher likelihood of FHA loan approval.
